The Founding
Three Friends. One Trip to Tuscany. One Obsession.
In 2012, Sikander Bakhtiar, Aamir Rashid, and Khurram Khan returned from Italy with a single mission — to bring stone-oven pizza and genuine Italian hospitality to Islamabad. No shortcuts. No compromises. Twelve years later, nothing has changed.
